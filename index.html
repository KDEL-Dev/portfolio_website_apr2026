ABOUT ME

My name is Kadelle Liburd and I am a student of the Humber College learning Web Development with the expected graduation date of May 2025. I am a graduate of the 3D animation program at Humber College and a graduate of the VFX for film and television program at Seneca College. I worked as a VFX compositor for the last 12 years. I've worked on various projects ranging from TV shows to Movies with some of them being, "Blue Clues and You", "Hunter Killer", "Alien Mysteries", and many more. With the experience I've gotten from the film and television industry, I hope to be able to use some of the skills I've learned there in conjunction with what I'm learning in Web Development.
